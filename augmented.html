Understanding Augmented Reality (AR)

Augmented reality (AR) isn't about replacing the real world, but rather enhancing it. Imagine looking through your smartphone camera and seeing a digital dinosaur towering over your park bench, or trying on new furniture virtually placed in your living room. AR overlays computer-generated images, sounds, and even sensory data onto the physical world you see. This creates an interactive experience where the digital and real seamlessly blend together.
